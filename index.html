ICT DEPARTMENT

In the department of ICT, we offer part time classes for computer packages. We also offer a two-year course known as COMPUTER OPERATOR examined by NITA.

In the department we offer ICT related services such as;

  • Photocopying
  • Lamination

  • Graphics design
  • KRA Returns
  • E-citizen online services
  • All Internet Services

ICT Lab

ICT Club session




The institution has a well-equipped Computer Laboratory with enough computers. The computers a fully networked with a Stable Wi-Fi internet connection with a very high speed. The Wi-Fi access is free for our trainees. The ICT facilities in the institution has enabled our instructors to Integrate ICT in Teaching and Learning process. This has enabled our learners to familiarize themselves with the current market demands and improve their training skills with an open mind of what the current modern society wants. The institution has a working and innovative ICT club members whereby the members use the ICT resources to improve their ICT skills as well as improve their creativity and innovation in IT related skills.
